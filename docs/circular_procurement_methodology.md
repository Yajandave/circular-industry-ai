# Circular Procurement and Supplier-Loop Methodology

Milestone 7E translates circular resolution plans into practical procurement actions. It is designed for supplier engagement, take-back discussions, reverse-logistics planning and contract evidence requests.

## Method
The engine combines:
- locked rules-engine recommendation
- circular resolution plan
- material-specific playbook
- supplier and current-route fields
- risk and evidence controls

It produces:
- procurement route
- supplier relationship type
- reverse-logistics model
- contract levers
- supplier questions
- supplier evidence requirements
- acceptance criteria
- commercial and operational checks
- procurement pilot scope
- review gate
- fallback position

## Controls
The supplier-loop layer does not verify supplier claims, legal waste status or circular impact. High-risk and human-review streams remain blocked from implementation until classification, contamination and authorised handling evidence is resolved.

## Circular procurement logic
Circular procurement is treated as more than buying recycled content. The engine also looks for supplier take-back, returnable packaging, specification changes, closed-loop material returns, reverse logistics, route evidence and value-chain data-sharing.

## Claim boundary
Supplier-loop plans are internal decision-support outputs. They must not be used as public claims until supplier evidence, route documentation and operational records are available.
